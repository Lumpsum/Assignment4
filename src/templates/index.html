About

Asian Dishes is a site about dishes that are available that range from vegetarian to illegal dishes. While using the application you can search for ingredients and the corresponding dishes so you can have a rough outline what the dish contains. With this you can make your own dishes and search for new dishes. Next to that you can also select a ingredient and find all the dishes that contain that ingredient. Have Fun!

Example Querying

Just a simple example of the querying to showcase the difference interferencing makes on this ontology.

PREFIX food: <http://www.semanticweb.org/rick/food#>

SELECT ?x WHERE {
  ?x a food:NonVegetarianDish .
}

Explanation of the results

As you can see the button with inferencing give you results and the right one does not, even though they use the same query, which is due inferencing. Inferencing looks inside your relationships and tries to make some relationship that you did not define yet but are present because of the restrictions etc. you gave to a class or property. In this example, the dishes are all classified as dishes but are not placed yet under vegetarian or non vegetarian for example. In other words, the left box displays the relationships he could make through my restrictions and the right one does not show anything because he did not make those relationship because the inferencing is disabled.

Pick an ingredient!

Here you can pick an ingredient on the list, click on the button and then you see all the dishes that contain that ingredient, so you can get some inspiration. If a dish is illegal in the western world, it will tell you as well so you know what you can and can't make.

PREFIX food: <http://www.semanticweb.org/rick/food#>

SELECT DISTINCT ?x 
WHERE {
  ?x a food:Ingredient
       }

Pick a dish!

Here you can pick a dish out of all the available dishes in the database. When you have chosen your dish you can click the button and all the ingredients will pop up on the right side!

SELECT DISTINCT ?x 
WHERE {
